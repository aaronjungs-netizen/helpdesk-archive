Symptom

  • Users encounter a "TronLink bandwidth error" when attempting to perform transactions or interact with the TRON network using TronLink, a popular wallet and browser extension.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to insufficient bandwidth or energy on the user's TRON account, which is required to process transactions on the network. This can be caused by a lack of TRX (TRON's native cryptocurrency) holdings or an insufficient bandwidth reservation.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase TRX holdings to reserve more bandwidth:
  • Purchase additional TRX to stake and reserve bandwidth
  • Delegate TRX to a witness or super representative to increase bandwidth
  • Use the following command to check bandwidth:
curl -X POST \
  https://api.trongrid.io/wallet/getaccount \
  -H 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  -d '{"address": "your_tron_address"}'

Consider using an external console for advanced troubleshooting and diagnostics.

Prerequisites

  • A basic understanding of the TRON ecosystem and its native cryptocurrency, TRX
  • A TronLink wallet with a registered account
  • A sufficient amount of TRX for bandwidth reservation and transaction fees
