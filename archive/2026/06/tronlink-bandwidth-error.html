Symptom

  • Users encounter a "TronLink bandwidth error" when attempting to perform transactions or interact with smart contracts on the TRON ecosystem, resulting in failed or stalled operations.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to insufficient bandwidth or energy on the user's TRON account, which is required to execute transactions and interact with smart contracts on the network.

  • Unfreeze TRX after a certain period to regain control over the tokens
  • Monitor bandwidth and energy levels regularly to avoid depletion
