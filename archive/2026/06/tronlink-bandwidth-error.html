Symptom

  • Users encounter a "TronLink bandwidth error" when attempting to perform transactions or interact with smart contracts on the TRON network using TronLink, a popular wallet and browser extension.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to insufficient bandwidth or energy on the TRON network, which is required to process transactions and execute smart contracts. This can be caused by a variety of factors, including high network congestion, insufficient TRX holdings, or incorrect wallet configuration.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase TRX holdings to acquire more bandwidth and energy
  • Adjust wallet settings to optimize bandwidth and energy usage
  • Use the following command to check available bandwidth:
tronweb.trx.getBalance("your_tron_address")

Consider utilizing an external console to execute these commands for better debugging.

Prerequisites

  • TRON wallet with sufficient TRX balance
  • TronLink browser extension installed and configured
  • Basic understanding of TRON network and transaction mechanics
