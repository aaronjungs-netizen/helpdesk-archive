Symptom

  • Users encounter a "TronLink bandwidth error" when attempting to perform transactions or interact with smart contracts on the TRON network using the TronLink wallet.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to insufficient bandwidth or energy on the user's account, which is required to process transactions on the TRON network. This can happen when a user has not acquired enough TRX tokens to freeze for bandwidth or has not waited for the bandwidth to replenish after recent transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • Check the current bandwidth and energy levels in the TronLink wallet to determine if they are sufficient for the desired transaction.
  • Freeze additional TRX tokens to increase the available bandwidth if necessary.

Prerequisites

  • A basic understanding of the TRON network and its resource management system.
  • Access to the TronLink wallet and its settings.
  • Sufficient TRX tokens to freeze for bandwidth or energy.
