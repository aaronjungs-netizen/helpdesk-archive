Symptom

  • Users encounter a "TronLink bandwidth error" when attempting to interact with the TRON ecosystem, such as sending transactions or deploying smart contracts. The error message indicates that the user's account has insufficient bandwidth to perform the requested action.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to TRON's bandwidth mechanism, which requires users to have a certain amount of bandwidth to perform transactions. Bandwidth is calculated based on the user's TRX holdings and the time since their last transaction. If a user's bandwidth is depleted, they must wait for it to replenish or acquire more TRX to increase their bandwidth.
