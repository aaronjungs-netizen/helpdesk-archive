Symptom

  • Users encounter a "TronLink bandwidth error" when attempting to perform transactions or interact with smart contracts on the TRON network, resulting in failed transactions and inability to execute desired actions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to insufficient bandwidth or energy on the user's TRON account, which is required to process transactions and execute smart contracts on the network. This can be caused by a lack of TRX holdings, insufficient bandwidth delegation, or network congestion.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase TRX holdings to acquire more bandwidth.
  • Delegate bandwidth to the account from another TRON account with sufficient bandwidth.
  • Use the following command to check available bandwidth:
trontools query account <account_address>

Consider utilizing an external console for more advanced troubleshooting and diagnostics.

Prerequisites

  • Basic understanding of TRON network and its transaction mechanics
  • Familiarity with TronLink wallet and its functionality
  • TRX holdings or access to a TRON account with sufficient bandwidth
