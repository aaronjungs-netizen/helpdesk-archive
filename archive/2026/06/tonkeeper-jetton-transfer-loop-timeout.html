Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to a prolonged waiting period for transaction confirmation, exceeding the predefined timeout threshold, often caused by network congestion or high transaction volumes.

Manual Overrides

  • Implement a retry mechanism to reattempt failed transactions
