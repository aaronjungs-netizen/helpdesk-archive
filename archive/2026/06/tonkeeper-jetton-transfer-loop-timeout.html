Symptom

  • Users experience a "Tonkeeper Jetton transfer loop timeout" error when attempting to transfer jettons on the TON ecosystem, resulting in failed transactions and frustration.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to a synchronization issue between the Tonkeeper wallet and the TON blockchain, causing the transfer loop to timeout and fail to complete the transaction.

Manual Overrides

  • Check the TON blockchain for any ongoing maintenance or issues that may be causing the synchronization problem.
  • Verify that the Tonkeeper wallet is updated to the latest version and configured correctly.
  • Use the following command to reset the transfer loop:
tonos-cli call 0:xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx setcode --input '{"timeout": 60}'

Consider utilizing an external console for more advanced troubleshooting and configuration options.

Prerequisites

  • Tonkeeper wallet installed and configured on the user's device
  • Latest version of the TON blockchain client
  • Stable internet connection for synchronization with the TON blockchain
