Symptom

  • Users of the Metamask extension on the Ethereum (ETH) ecosystem notice that their Stop-Loss (SL) and Take-Profit (TP) percentages are altered when they modify the leverage settings, despite no intentional changes being made to these parameters.

Technical Root Cause

  • The issue arises due to an incorrect handling of decimal places and percentage calculations within the Metamask extension's code when leverage is adjusted, leading to unintended modifications of the SL and TP values.
