Metamask Extension Bug Sl Tp Percentages Change When Changing L

Symptom

  • Users observe that the Stop-Loss (SL) and Take-Profit (TP) percentages in Metamask extension change unexpectedly when modifying the leverage.

Technical Root Cause

  • The issue arises due to a bug in the Metamask extension that incorrectly recalculates SL and TP percentages based on the updated leverage, instead of maintaining their original values.

Manual Overrides

  • Update the Metamask extension to the latest version
  • Reset the SL and TP percentages manually after changing the leverage
  • Use a custom script to maintain the original SL and TP percentages
