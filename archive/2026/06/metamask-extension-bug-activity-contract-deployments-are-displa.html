Metamask Extension Bug Activity Contract Deployments Are Displayed as Contract Interaction

Symptom

  • Users observe that contract deployments are incorrectly displayed as contract interactions within the Metamask extension, leading to confusion and potential mismanagement of on-chain activities.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to a bug in the Metamask extension's activity logging mechanism, where it fails to properly differentiate between contract deployment transactions and regular contract interaction transactions, resulting in the misclassification.

Manual Overrides

  • To temporarily resolve the issue, users can attempt the following manual overrides:
  • Manually refresh the Metamask extension to ensure the latest data is fetched.
  • Utilize the Ethereum blockchain explorer to verify the nature of the transactions.
  • Execute a script to filter out contract deployments, such as:
curl -X GET 'https://api.etherscan.io/api?module=account&action=txlist&address=0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc454e4438f44e&startblock=0&endblock=99999999&sort=asc' | jq '.result[] | select(.to == "0x0000000000000000000000000000000000000000")'
