  • The error occurs due to a temporary mismatch in the available payment routes configured in the Metamask extension, likely caused by updates or changes in the Ethereum (ETH) ecosystem.

  • Attempt to restart the Metamask extension and retry the transaction.

  "availableRoutes": ["mUSD", "USDT", "DAI"]
