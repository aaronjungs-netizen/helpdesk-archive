Metamask Nonce Out Of Sync

Symptom

  • Users experience failed transactions or warnings when attempting to send ETH or interact with smart contracts, with an error message indicating that the MetaMask nonce is out of sync.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs when the nonce (a counter that prevents transaction replay) stored in MetaMask does not match the nonce stored on the Ethereum blockchain, typically after a failed or pending transaction.

Manual Overrides

  • Reset the account in MetaMask to resync the nonce with the blockchain.
  • Manually adjust the nonce in the transaction settings to match the expected nonce.
  • Use the following JSON-RPC method to retrieve the current nonce:
{
  "jsonrpc": "2.0",
  "method": "eth_getTransactionCount",
  "params": ["0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc454e4438f44e", "latest"],
  "id": 1
}

Consider utilizing an external console for more advanced debugging and nonce management.

Prerequisites

  • MetaMask extension installed and configured.
  • An Ethereum wallet with a balance.
  • A basic understanding of Ethereum transactions and nonces.
