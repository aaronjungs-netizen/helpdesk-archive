Metamask Nonce Out Of Sync

Symptom

  • Users encounter an error message stating "MetaMask nonce out of sync" when attempting to send transactions or interact with smart contracts on the Ethereum network, indicating a discrepancy between the expected and actual nonce values.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to a mismatch between the nonce value stored in MetaMask and the actual nonce value on the Ethereum blockchain, often caused by cancelled or pending transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • Reset the account in MetaMask to reconcile the nonce value:
  • Update the MetaMask settings to automatically reset the account
  • Use the eth_accounts RPC method to retrieve the list of accounts
  • Execute the following command to reset the account nonce:
mm-reset-nonce --account <account_address>

Consider utilizing an external console for more complex transactions.

Prerequisites

  • MetaMask extension installed and configured
  • Ethereum account with a sufficient balance
  • Basic understanding of Ethereum transaction mechanics
