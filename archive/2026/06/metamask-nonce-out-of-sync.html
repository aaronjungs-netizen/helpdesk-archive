Symptom

  • Users experience a "nonce out of sync" error when attempting to send transactions using MetaMask on the Ethereum network, resulting in failed or stuck transactions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to a mismatch between the expected nonce (transaction count) stored in MetaMask and the actual nonce on the Ethereum blockchain, often caused by concurrent transactions or wallet resets.

Manual Overrides

  • Reset the MetaMask account nonce to match the on-chain value:
  • Check the current account nonce on Etherscan or a similar blockchain explorer
  • Update the MetaMask account settings to reflect the correct nonce
  • Use the following command to verify the nonce:
eth_getTransactionCount <account_address> --json

For a more seamless experience, consider utilizing an external console to execute complex commands.

Prerequisites

  • MetaMask wallet installed and configured
  • Basic understanding of Ethereum transactions and nonces
  • Access to Etherscan or a similar blockchain explorer
