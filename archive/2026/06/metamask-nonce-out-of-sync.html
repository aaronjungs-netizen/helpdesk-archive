Symptom

  • Users encounter an error message indicating that their MetaMask nonce is out of sync, preventing them from completing transactions on the Ethereum network.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs when the nonce (a counter that keeps track of the number of transactions sent from an address) stored in MetaMask does not match the nonce on the Ethereum blockchain, typically due to a missed or failed transaction.

Manual Overrides

  • Reset the account in MetaMask to synchronize the nonce with the blockchain state.
  • Use the Ethereum blockchain explorer to verify the current nonce and manually adjust it in MetaMask if necessary.

Prerequisites

  • MetaMask extension installed and configured.
  • Ethereum account with a history of transactions.
  • Access to an Ethereum blockchain explorer or API service like Infura.
