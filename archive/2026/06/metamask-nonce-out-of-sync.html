Symptom

  • The user encounters an error message stating "MetaMask nonce out of sync" when attempting to send a transaction on the Ethereum network.

Technical Root Cause

  • This error occurs due to a discrepancy between the expected and actual nonce values in MetaMask, which is responsible for tracking the sequence of transactions sent from a specific Ethereum account.
