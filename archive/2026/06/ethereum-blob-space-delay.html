Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed blob space transactions on the Ethereum network, resulting in incomplete data storage or retrieval.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network, causing a backlog of pending transactions and delaying the processing of blob space requests.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas price to prioritize the transaction: eth_sendTransaction({from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '0x...', gas: '0x...', gasPrice: '0x1234'})
  • Use a different transaction nonce to bypass stuck transactions
