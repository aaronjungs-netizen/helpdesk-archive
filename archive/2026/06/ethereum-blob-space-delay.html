Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions due to Ethereum blob space congestion, resulting in increased latency and potential timeouts.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs when the Ethereum network is overwhelmed with a high volume of transactions, causing a backlog in the blob space, which is responsible for storing and managing large data blobs.
