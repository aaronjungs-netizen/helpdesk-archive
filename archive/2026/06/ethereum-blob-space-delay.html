Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a backlog in blob space allocation.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network, specifically when the demand for blob space exceeds the available capacity, causing a delay in transaction processing.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas price to prioritize the transaction: eth_sendTransaction({from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '0x...', gas: '0x...', gasPrice: '0x...'})
