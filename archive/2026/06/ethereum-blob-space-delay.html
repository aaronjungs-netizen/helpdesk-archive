Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, often resulting in increased gas fees and slower than expected processing times. The error may be accompanied by a warning message indicating a delay in the blob space.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network, often caused by a high volume of transactions and limited block space. This congestion leads to a backlog of transactions waiting to be processed, resulting in delays.

Manual Overrides

  • To mitigate the delay, users can try the following:
  • Increase the gas price to prioritize their transactions
  • Use a different transaction routing strategy to avoid congested nodes
  • Implement a retry mechanism with exponential backoff to handle failed transactions
