Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed blob space allocation on the Ethereum network, resulting in slower than expected transaction processing times and potential timeouts.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to a temporary backlog in the Ethereum network's blob space allocation mechanism, often caused by high traffic volumes or network congestion.
