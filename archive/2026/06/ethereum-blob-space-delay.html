Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar issues, resulting in frustrated users and potential financial losses.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs when the network is congested, and the gas prices are too high, causing transactions to be stuck in the mempool, unable to be processed by miners due to insufficient gas.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas price to a competitive level to incentivize miners to process the transaction:
  • Use a gas price estimation tool to determine the optimal gas price
  • Manually set the gas price using a command-line interface or a wallet
  • Monitor the transaction status and adjust the gas price as needed

Prerequisites

  • Access to a command-line interface or a compatible wallet
