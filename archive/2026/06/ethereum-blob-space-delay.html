Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed transactions and increased latency when interacting with the Ethereum network, resulting in failed or pending transactions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum blob space, which is responsible for storing and managing large data blobs associated with transactions. This congestion can be caused by a high volume of transactions, network congestion, or inefficient data storage.

Manual Overrides

  • To resolve the issue, try the following manual overrides:
  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize transactions and reduce congestion.
  • Use a different transaction routing strategy to bypass congested nodes.
  • Optimize data storage by compressing or splitting large data blobs, as shown in the example below:
