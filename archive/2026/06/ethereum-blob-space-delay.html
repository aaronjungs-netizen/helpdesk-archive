Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar issues, resulting in slow processing times for their transactions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to excessive usage of blob space on the Ethereum network, which can happen when there's a surge in demand for storage or when contracts excessively utilize this space. This leads to congestion and delays in transaction processing.
