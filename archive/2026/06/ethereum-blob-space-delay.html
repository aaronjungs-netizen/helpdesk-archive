Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a blob space delay. The delay manifests as a prolonged processing time for transactions, causing inconvenience to users who rely on timely transaction execution.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs due to network congestion and the limited capacity of the Ethereum blockchain to process transactions efficiently. When the network is overloaded with transactions, the available blob space is depleted, causing delays in transaction processing.

Manual Overrides

  • To mitigate the delay, users can attempt the following manual overrides:
  • Increase the gas price to prioritize transaction processing
  • Split large transactions into smaller ones to reduce network load
  • Use a transaction accelerator service to expedite processing
