Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar issues, resulting in slowed or halted interaction with smart contracts and decentralized applications.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs due to congestion and high usage of the Ethereum network, leading to a backlog of transactions waiting to be processed. This congestion can be exacerbated by factors such as high gas prices, network spam, or an influx of new users.
