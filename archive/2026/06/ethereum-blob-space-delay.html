Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating an Ethereum blob space delay. The delay affects the processing and confirmation of transactions, causing frustration and uncertainty for users.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs due to excessive demand for blob space, exceeding the available capacity. This leads to a backlog of transactions waiting to be processed, resulting in delays.
