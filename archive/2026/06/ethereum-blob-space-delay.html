Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar wording, often resulting in prolonged wait times for transaction confirmations.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network, specifically when the blob space, which is used for storing and transmitting large data, is overwhelmed with requests, leading to delays in processing transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • To mitigate the issue, users can try the following:
  • Increase the gas price to prioritize their transactions.
  • Use a different Ethereum node or provider to potentially bypass the congestion.
