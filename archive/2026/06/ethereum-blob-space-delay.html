Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or pending transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a blob space delay. The delay affects the overall performance and usability of the network, causing frustration among users.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to the limited capacity of the Ethereum blob space, which is a storage system for large data files. When the blob space reaches its maximum capacity, new transactions are delayed until space becomes available, resulting in a backlog of pending transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize transactions: eth_sendTransaction({from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '1 ether', gas: '2000000'})
  • Use a different storage solution, such as IPFS, to reduce the load on the Ethereum blob space
  • Implement a queue system to manage pending transactions and optimize network usage
