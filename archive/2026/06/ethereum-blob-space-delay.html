Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating an "Ethereum blob space delay".

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to excessive blob data being stored on the Ethereum blockchain, causing a delay in processing new transactions. This happens when the network is congested and the gas prices are high, leading to a backlog of unprocessed transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas price to prioritize the transaction: eth_sendTransaction({from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '0x...', gas: '0x...', gasPrice: '0x...'})
  • Use a different transaction routing mechanism to bypass congested nodes
  • Implement a retry mechanism with exponential backoff to handle temporary delays
