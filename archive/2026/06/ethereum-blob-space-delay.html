Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar issues, resulting in frustrated users and stalled interactions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs when the network is congested, and the gas prices are too high, causing transactions to be delayed or stuck in the mempool, awaiting processing.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas price to prioritize the transaction: eth_sendTransaction({from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '1 ether', gasPrice: '20000000000'})
  • Use a transaction accelerator service to bump the gas price
  • Implement a retry mechanism with exponential backoff to handle temporary congestion
