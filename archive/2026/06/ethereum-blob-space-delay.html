Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed blob space transactions on the Ethereum network, resulting in timeouts or unconfirmed transactions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network, causing a backlog of unprocessed transactions and delaying the allocation of blob space.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas price to prioritize the transaction: eth_sendTransaction {from: '0x...', to: '0x...', value: '0x...', gas: '0x...', gasPrice: '0x1234'}
  • Use a separate transaction to allocate blob space before sending the main transaction
  • Implement a retry mechanism with exponential backoff to handle temporary network congestion
