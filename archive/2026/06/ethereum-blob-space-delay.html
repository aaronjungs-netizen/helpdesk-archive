Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a delay in blob space availability.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion in the Ethereum network, caused by a high volume of transactions competing for limited blob space, leading to delayed processing and confirmation of transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • To mitigate the issue, users can try the following:
  • Increase the gas price to prioritize their transactions
  • Use a different transaction routing mechanism to avoid congested nodes
  • Implement a retry mechanism with exponential backoff to handle temporary delays
