Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a "blob space delay" or similar issues related to excessive data storage.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to the Ethereum network's limited storage capacity for blob data, which is used to store and manage large amounts of data, such as images and other multimedia files. When this capacity is exceeded, transactions are delayed until space becomes available.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize transactions and potentially bypass the delay:
  • Increase the gasLimit in the transaction parameters
  • Use a higher gasPrice to incentivize miners to process the transaction sooner
  • Implement a retry mechanism with exponential backoff to handle temporary delays
