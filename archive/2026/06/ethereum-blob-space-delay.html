Symptom

  • Users experience delays in accessing and retrieving data from Ethereum blob storage, resulting in slow transaction processing times and potential timeouts.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs due to network congestion, high gas prices, and inefficient data storage mechanisms, leading to slower data retrieval and transaction verification times.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit and gas price to prioritize transactions and reduce congestion.
  • Optimize data storage by using compression and caching mechanisms to reduce the amount of data being stored and retrieved.
  • Utilize layer 2 scaling solutions to offload data storage and processing from the main Ethereum network, reducing congestion and increasing throughput.
