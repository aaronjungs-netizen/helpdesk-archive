Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed transactions and high gas fees when interacting with the Ethereum network, resulting in slow processing times for their transactions.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to congestion on the Ethereum network, which causes a backlog of unprocessed transactions, leading to delays in the blob space.

Manual Overrides

  • To resolve the issue, users can attempt to:
  • Increase the gas price to prioritize their transactions
  • Use a different Ethereum node to reduce congestion
  • Implement a retry mechanism with exponential backoff to handle failed transactions
