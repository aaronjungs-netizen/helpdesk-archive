Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating an Ethereum blob space delay.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to the limited storage capacity for blob data on the Ethereum network, which can lead to congestion and delays when a large number of users attempt to store or retrieve data.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize transactions and alleviate congestion.
  • Use a third-party service to optimize blob data storage and reduce the load on the network.
  • Implement a custom solution using the following code:
eth_sendBlob("0x...blob_data...", { from: "0x...user_address...", gas: "0x...increased_gas..." })

Consider utilizing an external console for more advanced troubleshooting and configuration options.

Prerequisites

  • A basic understanding of Ethereum network architecture
  • Familiarity with gas management and transaction prioritization
  • Access to a compatible Ethereum client or third-party service
