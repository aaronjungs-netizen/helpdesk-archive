Ethereum Blob Space Delay

Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating a blob space delay. The delay affects the overall performance and efficiency of the network, causing frustration among users.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs due to the limited capacity of the Ethereum blockchain to process and store large amounts of data, such as blobs. When the network is congested, and multiple users attempt to transfer or store large files, it leads to a delay in processing these transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • To resolve the issue, users can try the following manual overrides:
  • Increase the gas limit to prioritize transactions and reduce congestion.
  • Use off-chain storage solutions to reduce the load on the Ethereum blockchain.
  • Implement a retry mechanism with exponential backoff using the following code:
while true; do
  eth_sendTransaction --gas 2000000 --value 0x1 --data 0x1234567890
  sleep 10
done
