Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs due to the limited storage capacity of the Ethereum blockchain, which leads to congestion and slower processing of transactions. This congestion is exacerbated by the increasing demand for Ethereum-based services and the limited scalability of the network.
