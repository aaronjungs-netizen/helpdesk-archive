Symptom

  • Users experience delayed or failed transactions on the Ethereum network, with error messages indicating an Ethereum blob space delay. This issue is characterized by slow processing times and potential timeouts.

Technical Root Cause

  • The Ethereum blob space delay occurs due to the network's limited capacity to process large amounts of data, particularly when dealing with blob-related transactions. This limitation can lead to congestion and delays in the network.

  • Optimize transaction sizes to minimize the amount of data being processed.
