Symptom

  • Users encounter a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error" when attempting to execute smart contracts or perform transactions on the TRON network, resulting in failed transactions and potential loss of funds.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in energy consumption by TRC-20 smart contracts, exceeding the allocated energy limits and causing the transaction to fail.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the energy limit for the specific smart contract to accommodate the increased energy requirements.
  • Optimize the smart contract code to reduce energy consumption and prevent future spikes.
  • Adjust the transaction parameters to prioritize energy efficiency:
tronweb.transactionBuilder.freezeBalance(10000, 3, 'TRX');

Consider utilizing an external console for more advanced troubleshooting and configuration options.

Prerequisites

  • A basic understanding of TRON's energy system and smart contract functionality
  • Access to the TRONWeb API or a compatible wallet
  • Familiarity with Solidity programming language for smart contract development
