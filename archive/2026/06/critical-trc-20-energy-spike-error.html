Symptom

  • Users encounter a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error" when interacting with TRC-20 tokens on the TRON network, resulting in failed transactions and significant disruptions to their activities.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in energy consumption by TRC-20 smart contracts, exceeding the allocated energy limits and causing the contracts to fail.

Manual Overrides

  • Increase the energy limit for the specific TRC-20 contract to accommodate the increased energy requirements.
  • Optimize the contract's code to reduce energy consumption and prevent future spikes.
  • Use the following command to update the contract's energy settings:
tronweb.transactionBuilder.updateEnergyLimit("contract_address", 1000)

Consider utilizing an external console for more complex contract adjustments.

Prerequisites

  • A basic understanding of TRON's energy system and its impact on smart contracts.
  • Familiarity with the TRONWeb library and its API.
  • Access to the TRON network and the specific TRC-20 contract in question.
