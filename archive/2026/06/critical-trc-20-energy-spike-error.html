Critical Trc 20 Energy Spike Error

Symptom

  • The user encounters a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error" message when attempting to initiate a transaction on the TRON network, indicating an abnormal increase in energy consumption by a TRC-20 smart contract.

Technical Root Cause

  • This error occurs due to an unexpected surge in energy usage, often caused by inefficient contract design, excessive loop iterations, or an unusually high volume of concurrent transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • To mitigate the issue, try the following steps:
  • Reduce the number of concurrent transactions to prevent overloading the network.
  • Optimize smart contract code to minimize energy consumption, focusing on loops and recursive functions.
