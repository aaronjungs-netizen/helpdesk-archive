Technical Root Cause

  • This error occurs due to an imbalance in the energy allocation for smart contracts, often caused by inefficient code or an unexpected increase in transaction volume, leading to a rapid depletion of available energy.

Manual Overrides

  • Adjust the energy limit for the smart contract to prevent excessive energy consumption:
  • Increase the energy_limit parameter in the contract deployment script
  • Implement energy-efficient coding practices to reduce consumption
  • Monitor and analyze energy usage patterns to identify areas for optimization
