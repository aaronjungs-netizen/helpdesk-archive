Critical Trc 20 Energy Spike Error

Symptom

  • Users experience a sudden and significant increase in energy consumption when attempting to transfer TRC-20 tokens on the TRON network, resulting in transaction failures and error messages indicating a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error".

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an imbalance between the energy limits set by the user and the actual energy required for the transaction, often caused by network congestion or inefficient smart contract code.

Manual Overrides

  • Adjust the energy limit for the transaction using the --energy flag.
  • Optimize smart contract code to reduce energy consumption.
  • Use a transaction builder to manually set the energy limit, as shown in the following example:
{
  "from": "0x...",
  "to": "0x...",
  "value": 0,
  "energy": 10000,
  "energyPrice": 1
}
