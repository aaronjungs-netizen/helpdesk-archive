Critical Trc 20 Energy Spike Error

Symptom

  • Users encounter a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error" message when attempting to perform transactions on the TRON network, specifically with TRC-20 tokens. The error prevents the transaction from completing, resulting in failed transfers and potential financial losses.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in energy consumption on the TRON network, often caused by a rapid increase in transaction volume or malicious activity. This surge exceeds the allocated energy limits for TRC-20 transactions, triggering the error.

Manual Overrides

  • To resolve the issue, users can attempt the following:
  • Increase the transaction energy limit using the --energy flag.
  • Implement a retry mechanism with exponential backoff to handle temporary energy spikes.
  • Utilize a TRON network explorer to monitor energy usage and plan transactions during periods of low network activity.
