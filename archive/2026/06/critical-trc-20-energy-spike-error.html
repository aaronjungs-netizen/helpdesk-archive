Symptom

  • Users experience a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error" when attempting to execute smart contracts or transfer tokens on the TRON network, resulting in failed transactions and significant energy consumption.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in energy consumption by a TRC-20 token contract, exceeding the maximum allowed energy limit, which is a critical parameter in the TRON Virtual Machine (TVM).

Manual Overrides

  • Adjust the energyLimit parameter in the contract deployment script to a higher value to accommodate the increased energy requirements.
  • Implement a custom energyPrice feed to dynamically adjust the energy cost based on network conditions.
  • Optimize contract code to reduce computational complexity and energy consumption:
trontools contract update --energy_limit 1000000 --contract_address 0x...

Consider utilizing an external console for more advanced contract debugging and optimization.

Prerequisites

  • TRON network node setup with TVM enabled
  • TRC-20 token contract deployment script
  • trontools command-line interface installed and configured
