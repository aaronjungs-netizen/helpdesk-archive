Symptom

  • Users experience a sudden and significant increase in energy consumption when interacting with TRC-20 tokens on the TRON network, resulting in failed transactions and error messages indicating a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error".

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an imbalance in the network's energy allocation, often caused by a surge in demand for TRC-20 transactions, which exceeds the available energy capacity of the user's account or the network's overall energy limits.
