Symptom

  • Users experience a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error" when attempting to execute smart contracts or perform transactions on the TRON network, resulting in failed transactions and significant energy consumption.

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in energy consumption by TRC-20 smart contracts, exceeding the allocated energy limits and causing the network to reject further transactions.

Manual Overrides

  • Adjust the energy limits for the specific smart contract to prevent excessive consumption:
  • Increase the energy_limit parameter in the contract's configuration
  • Implement energy-efficient coding practices to reduce consumption
  • Monitor energy usage in real-time using the following command:
trongrid.py contract getenergy <contract_address>

Consider utilizing an external console for more advanced debugging and monitoring capabilities.

Prerequisites

  • TRON network node setup and configuration
  • Familiarity with TRC-20 smart contract development and deployment
  • Basic understanding of energy management on the TRON network
