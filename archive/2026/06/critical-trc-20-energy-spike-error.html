Symptom

  • The user encounters a "Critical TRC-20 energy spike error" message, indicating an abnormal increase in energy consumption by a TRC-20 smart contract on the TRON network.

Technical Root Cause

  • This error occurs when a TRC-20 smart contract exceeds the maximum allowed energy limit, causing the contract execution to fail and resulting in the error message.

Manual Overrides

  • Adjust the contract's energy limits by modifying the energyLimit parameter in the contract's configuration.
  • Optimize the contract's code to reduce energy consumption by minimizing computational complexity.
