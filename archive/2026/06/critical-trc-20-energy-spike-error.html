Symptom

Technical Root Cause

  • The error occurs due to an unexpected surge in network activity, causing the energy requirements for transaction processing to exceed the available resources, leading to a critical energy spike.

  • Adjust the transaction energy limit using the --energy flag when initiating a transfer.

  • Utilize a custom configuration script, such as the following:

  "transaction": {
    "energyLimit": 1000000
  }
}
